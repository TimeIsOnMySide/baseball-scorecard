How do I keep score?

Start a new game, enter your teams and lineups, then tap any cell on the scorecard to record a play — hits, outs, walks, strikeouts, errors, and more. The app tracks runs, hits, and outs automatically, inning by inning, and follows runners around the diamond for you.

I'm new to scorekeeping — where do I start?

Open the Learn tab. It teaches you to read a scorecard from scratch, with every symbol, field position, and notation explained using real examples. It's also a handy refresher for veterans.

How do I share or print a game?

Open any finished game and choose Export. You can save it as a polished PDF or image that includes the full scorecard, line score, and batting stats — ready to send, print, or keep as a memento.

What is Scorecard Pro?

Scorecard Pro is an optional one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited saved games, every accent color, and custom inning counts from 3 to 12. It's yours forever — there is no subscription. The free version lets you start scoring right away.

I bought Scorecard Pro but it's not unlocked. How do I restore it?

Open the unlock screen and tap Restore purchases. Your purchase is tied to your App Store account and can be restored on any device signed into that account. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy it.

How do I get a refund?

All purchases are handled by Apple. Refund requests are managed through the App Store at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Where is my data stored? Do I need an account?

No account, no sign-up, no internet required. All your games and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere. The app works completely offline. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Will I lose my games if I delete the app?

Yes — because everything is stored only on your device, deleting the app removes its data. Export any games you want to keep as a PDF or image before uninstalling.
