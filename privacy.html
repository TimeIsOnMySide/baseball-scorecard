Privacy Policy

Baseball Scorecard · Last updated: June 6, 2026

Baseball Scorecard ("the App") is a personal baseball scorecard tool built for fans who want to keep score on their phone. This policy describes what data the App collects (almost none) and how it's handled.

Data we collect

The App stores your scorecards, game settings, and preferences locally on your device only, using SQLite and device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any advertising identifiers

In-app purchases

The App offers a one-time optional premium unlock ("Scorecard Pro"). Purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We never receive or store your payment information. All purchase verification is handled by the platform's native billing system.

Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

Data retention

All app data is stored locally and remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. We have no ability to access or delete data on your device.

Children

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no data transmitted off-device.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
